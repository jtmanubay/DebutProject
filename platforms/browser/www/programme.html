Sequence of Events

5:40 - 6:00pm
Cocktail Time
Guests can munch on appetizers before the actual dinner while waiting. May also enjoy the photo booths too!
6:00 - 6:20pm
Welcome Remarks, Debutante's Grand Entrance
Setting the tone of the evening. Debutante's creative entrance that complements the party's theme!
6:20 - 6:40pm
Cotillion de Honor
Performance of the Cotillion de Honor with the entourage. One of the highlights of the evening!
6:40 - 7:40pm
Dinner
Guests may now enjoy their meal with pleasingly graceful background music and a band to play soothing songs too.
7:40 - 8:20pm
The 18 Roses, Candles, Treasures, and Bills
Participants may now dance, give their gifts and messages to the debutante.
8:20 - 8:40pm
Games and Intermission Numbers
Keeping everyone's energy high. Playing games and showcasing talents!
8:40 - 8:50pm
Happy Birthday Song, Debutante's Speech
Debutante's long speech- a heartfelt one to thank and value everyone.
8:50 - 10:00pm
Closing Remarks and Party!
Ending the debut with a bang! Everyone may take the dance floor and dance the night away!
