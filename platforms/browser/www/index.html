Lechon | Meat Dish

Meat dish, a whole pig, marinated and roasted over charcoal. Prepared and stuffed with herbs which include scallions, bay leaves, garlic, salt, lemongrass and other spices.

Chicken Cordon Bleu | Chicken Dish

Dish of chicken meat wrapped around a slice of cheese and slices of ham, then breaded and deep-fried.

Pancit Bihon Guisado | Noodle Dish

Stir-fried vermicelli noodles with vegetables and pork.

Kare Kare | Stew Dish

A meat, tripe, and oxtail stew with vegetables in peanut sauce and served with bagoong alamang or shrimp paste.
